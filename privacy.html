Mit Bootcamp Alumni Bot

A bot to get information about the MIT Entrepreneurship Bootcamp Alumni (only for members of the Alumni Community)

Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

We collect minimal information from you when you use the bot. We only take your first name and last name from facebook and take your email to verify if you are a member of the MIT bootcamp Alumni.

What do we use the information for?

The information (name,email) we collect is used in the following ways

  1. Name

    We use your name to provide you a more personal messaging experience

  2. Email

    We use your email to verify that you are a member of the MIT bootcamp alumni

Do we disclose your information to third parties?

No. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. Other members of the alumni community will however be able to access information about you from the alumni database.

Your Consent
