Mit Bootcamp Alumni Bot

A bot to get information about the MIT Entrepreneurship Bootcamp Alumni (only for members of the Alumni Community)

Features

  • Search By Skills

    You can search and find alumni by a skillset (eg: find people good at sales)

  • Search By Location

    You can search and find alumni by their current Location (eg: Find people in India)
